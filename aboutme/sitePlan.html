Purpose

This site is to provide an introduction of who I am and my credentials to interested parties. This is to document information about me being the author, being considered for project or for employment. It will house links to sites that I find useful as well as examples of my work. This site will be able to be viewed on desktop, laptop, tablet, or mobile.

Audience

This site is targeted to both professional and non-professional viewers. This is for users of my web sites, prospective clients, partners or employers.
