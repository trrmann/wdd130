Overview

Purpose

This website is intended to function as an artistic portfolio in such a way that it shows the services and works offered by Luchonio as a personal brand, which specializes in cartoon drawings. The services offered range from character creation to logo designs, all always hand in hand with the client, in turn also seeking to publicize some personal comic and animation projects that are being developed in the future.

Audience

Adults, between 20 and 35 years old, who are looking for an art style that resembles the series they saw in their childhood and at the same time follows current trends through a cartoon style. In addition, it is aimed at those who seek to graph any idea they have, be it from a logo, comic, character design; and turn the stories they have in mind into reality in order to make them known to the public either through social networks or in print.
